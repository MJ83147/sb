Directions to the venue

From the Western Entrance

Near Customs House Elizabeth line, The Fox Bar, the dock

Face away from ExCeL and walk down the steps. The Sunborn yacht will be on your left, the Novotel straight ahead. Walk to the right of the building to the entrance, take the lift or stairs to the first floor. Walk left at reception and turn to face the bar. The King George room is to the left of the bar.

From the Eastern Entrance

Near Aloft hotel and bar, Prince Regent DLR station

Enter ExCeL and walk the length of the building. Once you exit at the Western entrance, walk down the steps. The Sunborn yacht will be on your left, the Novotel straight ahead. Walk to the right of the building to the entrance, take the lift or stairs to the first floor. Walk left at reception, face the bar. The King George room is to the left of the bar.
